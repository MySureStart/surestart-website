Vibe Lab: AI for Building Your First Social Enterprise

Turn your ideas into apps that make a difference.

Starts January 10, 2026 | Live Online | Early-Bird $695

Secure Your Spot

Create with Vibe Coding

Come join our 6-week (Saturdays only) instructor-led and mentor-supported AI course, where you'll go from idea to launch — creating your first social enterprise app using Vibe Coding, a new way of building with AI that lets you code at the speed of thought. No prior coding experience required!

Register Now Get a sneak peek

You'll learn how to:

  • Use Bolt, a leading vibe coding tool, to build real tech products
  • Understand the tech frontend, backend, and integrations that power apps
  • Develop an entrepreneurial mindset and use AI tools to turn ideas into reality
  • Build durable skills including analytical and critical reasoning to tackle complex challenges
  • Strengthen leadership and communication abilities that will set you up for future success
  • Work in teams of 3 to launch your first social enterprise and pitch it at a virtual Symposium.

Who it's for:

  • High school and college students, who want to learn how to Vibe code effectively, build apps, solve real problems, and explore the intersection of AI, entrepreneurship, and social good.

How it works:

  • Each weekend, students join a 2-hour instructor-led live session and 1-hour team project guided by expert mentors (1:10 ratio), with ongoing async support and collaboration via Discord.

By the end of the course, you'll have:

Session Dates Module Session Description
1/10/2026 Module 1: Intro to Vibe-Coding for Social Impact Learn to turn ideas into applications using AI-powered Vibe Coding tools. Explore UN Sustainable Development Goals, and build your first Vibe Coded app to address a SDG-aligned challenge.
1/17/2026 Module 2: Community-Centered Design & Social Entrepreneurship Apply human-first design and entrepreneurial thinking to solve real-world problems. Learn to apply user feedback and ethical frameworks to shape strong product–market fit.
1/24/2026 Module 3: Collaborative Development and Resource Management Prototype Vibe Coding apps to address real-world challenges, while exploring principles of successful collaboration and lean resource management through an understanding of token economics.
1/31/2026 Module 4: Backend Integration & Sustainable Solutions Discover how data, authentication, and backend systems power Vibe-coded apps to ensure security, scalability, and sustainable functionality.
2/07/2026 Module 5: Hands-on Project Development for Social Impact Bring your project to life through collaborative development with your team, and iterative refinement using Vibe tools. Learn to pitch and market your social impact solution.
2/14/2026 Optional Office Hours – Project Review Meet with instructors for feedback, troubleshooting, and presentation rehearsal before the final showcase.
2/21/2026 Module 6: Showcase & Reflection Present your completed Vibe Coding–based social impact project and reflections on your learning journey to peers, parents and invited guests.
