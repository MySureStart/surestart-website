Vibe Lab: AI for Building Your First Social Enterprise

Turn your ideas into apps that make a difference.

Starts January 10, 2026 | Live Online | Early-Bird $695

Secure your spot

Create with Vibe Coding

Come join our 6-week (Saturdays only) instructor-led and mentor-supported AI course, where you'll go from idea to launch — creating your first social enterprise app using Vibe Coding, a new way of building with AI that lets you code at the speed of thought. No prior coding experience required!

Join the interest list Get a sneak peek

You'll learn how to:

  • Use Bolt, a leading vibe coding tool, to build real tech products
  • Understand the tech frontend, backend, and integrations that power apps
  • Develop an entrepreneurial mindset and use AI tools to turn ideas into reality
  • Build durable skills including analytical and critical reasoning to tackle complex challenges
  • Strengthen leadership and communication abilities that will set you up for future success
  • Work in teams of 3 to launch your first social enterprise and pitch it at a virtual Symposium.

Who it's for:

  • High school and college students, who want to learn how to Vibe code effectively, build apps, solve real problems, and explore the intersection of AI, entrepreneurship, and social good.

How it works:

  • Each weekend, students join a 2-hour instructor-led live session and 1-hour team project guided by expert mentors (1:10 ratio), with ongoing async support and collaboration via Discord.

By the end of the course, you'll have:

✓
A fully functional app you built using Vibe Coding
✓
Experienced the startup mindset through collaborative innovation
✓
Gained awareness of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
✓
Developed a proof-of-concept for a social enterprise aligned with a UN SDG
✓
Built confidence to launch, pitch, and grow your impact

The Details:

When:

1pm ET sessions on Saturdays beginning January 10 — February 21, 2026

Format:

Six 2-hour live Saturday sessions and six 1-hour independent challenges to be completed with your teammates or independently between sessions

Price:

Early-bird $695 | Standard $795

Mentorship:

1:10 mentor-to-student ratio

Showcase:

Pitch your prototype at The Social Enterprise Symposium

*No prior coding experience required!
