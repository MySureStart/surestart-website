# Product Context: SureStart Website

## What This Product Does

The SureStart website is the primary digital presence for an AI education company. It serves as:

1. **Marketing Platform** - Attracts K-12 schools, higher education institutions, and individual students
2. **Information Hub** - Explains programs, courses, and partnership opportunities
3. **Lead Generation** - Contact forms and CTAs to drive inquiries
4. **Social Proof** - Showcases impact stories, testimonials, and partner logos

## Target Audiences

### Primary Audiences

| Audience | Pages | Key Actions |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| **K-12 Schools** | k12.html, contact-us.html | Request partnership info, schedule call |
| **Higher Ed Institutions** | higher-ed.html, contact-us.html | Explore programs, contact for partnership |
| **Students** | students.html, index.html | Learn about courses, join Discord community |

### Secondary Audiences

- **Parents** - Understanding program value for their children
- **Educators/Teachers** - Professional development opportunities
- **Media/Press** - About Us page for company information

## Key User Journeys

### Journey 1: K-12 Administrator
1. Lands on Home or K-12 page (search/referral)
2. Reviews program benefits and differentiators
3. Reads success stories for social proof
4. Clicks "Schedule a Call" CTA
5. Fills out contact form

### Journey 2: College/University Partner
1. Lands on Higher Ed page
2. Reviews partnership models and outcomes
3. Views case studies (PAS4AI, Break Through Tech)
4. Contacts for partnership discussion

### Journey 3: Student
1. Discovers via social media or search
2. Explores Students page for offerings
3. Joins Discord community
4. Signs up for course notifications

## Content Priorities

1. **Credibility** - Partner logos, university acceptances, internship placements
2. **Impact** - Statistics, testimonials, student success stories
3. **Programs** - Clear descriptions of course offerings
4. **Action** - Strong CTAs to contact or sign up

## Brand Voice

- **Empowering** - Focus on student potential and outcomes
- **Inclusive** - Emphasis on diversity in AI/tech
- **Expert** - Founded by AI scientist with industry credibility
- **Forward-Looking** - Future-proofing skills for AI-driven world
