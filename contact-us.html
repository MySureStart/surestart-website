Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and enrollment process.

Getting started is easy! Simply visit our programs page, choose the course that matches your experience level, and click "Get Started." You'll be guided through our simple enrollment process, which includes a brief application and program selection.

Our programs are designed for middle and high school students, typically ages 12-18. We offer different tracks based on experience level, from complete beginners to advanced students ready for college-level AI concepts.

Our programs range from 8 to 20 weeks depending on the track. Students typically spend 3-5 hours per week on coursework, including live sessions with mentors, project work, and peer collaboration. We design our programs to fit around school schedules.
