Program length and weekly time commitment vary because SureStart's curriculum is fully modular and tailored to the needs of each partner school or organization. We work closely with partners to design programs that fit naturally within existing schedules—whether that means shorter exploratory workshops, semester-long courses, or intensive project-based experiences. Students typically engage in a mix of live instruction, hands-on projects, and collaborative work, with the time commitment adjusted to match the program design.

Our instructional team and mentors provide guidance throughout the program to help every student build confidence, develop skills, and succeed in their learning journey.
