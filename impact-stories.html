"Participating in SureStart was the single best decision I have made in my academic career. As a student from a background underrepresented in Machine Learning, the program provided a supportive and comfortable springboard into all things AI. The mentorship was fantastic and I have continued to keep in contact with my SureStart mentors for years! My interest in ML was cemented after completing SureStart, so I decided to pursue a bachelors degree in Mathematics at Stanford. I graduated this past June, and I am now starting my Computer Science PhD at Princeton. I would highly recommend SureStart to any young student, it truly changed my life!"
"Being a part of the MIT FutureMakers Create-a-thon* has genuinely been a once in a lifetime experience. Not only have I learned fantastic technical and power skills, but I've strengthened my network, met inspiring mentors and peers, and broadened my concept of what it means to be a woman in STEM. Oftentimes, I feel as though I take up too much space in a room or I must apologize for every mistake. I can't seem to shake off this idea that I'm too young, inexperienced, and feminine to belong in a space dedicated to CS or any other male dominated field. Yet, I never felt that way in the MIT FutureMakers program. From our very first day, SureStart created a welcoming and nourishing environment, where women like me are not the exception but the majority. We were encouraged to learn things we had never dreamed of, ask questions when we didn't understand, and most importantly, request help when we needed it. Our conversations about ethical AI made sure not to ignore the burdens we carry, and discussed how to prevent gender-based inequities. Our guest speakers taught us how to voice our unique stories, goals, and skills. SureStart made this possible and I am so grateful not only for the opportunity to participate in this program, but to be part of this group of empowering women."
